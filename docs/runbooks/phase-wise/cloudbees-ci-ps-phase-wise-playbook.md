# CloudBees CI PS Phase-Wise Playbook and Runbook

## Purpose
This master runbook converts the CloudBees CI Professional Services onboarding checklist into an execution-oriented playbook.

The goal is to prove that the consultant can design, install, configure, troubleshoot, migrate, automate, explain, and demonstrate CloudBees CI in customer-facing Professional Services engagements.

## Evidence Standard
Each phase should capture CLI outputs, screenshots, service status, URLs, known issues, runbook notes, and mentor/manager sign-off.

## Completion Definition
The onboarding is complete when the consultant can independently design, install, configure, troubleshoot, migrate, automate, and explain CloudBees CI across Traditional and Modern patterns.
